A Labor of Love Built by Italian Dreamers

In a pioneering location where few dared to build, an Italian couple envisioned something extraordinary—a European villa rising from the Baja wilderness.

This is not just another luxury property. This is a testament to vision, passion, and uncompromising dedication. The owners shipped an entire container of premium windows from Germany. They hand-selected Saltillo tiles crafted in Dolores Hidalgo. Every door, every finish, every architectural detail was chosen with the care of artisans building their life's masterpiece.

Built in 2007 when Las Playitas was still undiscovered, this estate now sits in one of Todos Santos' most privileged positions—commanding dual views of the Pacific Ocean and the Sierra de la Laguna mountains, surrounded by ancient cardon forests and meticulously designed landscaping that feels like a Mediterranean garden.

After years of cherished memories, the owners are ready for their next chapter. This villa awaits its next visionary—whether a family seeking an unparalleled coastal retreat, an entrepreneur envisioning a boutique hotel, or a creative soul drawn to host transformative experiences in this magical setting.
